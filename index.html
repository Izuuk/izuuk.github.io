About Me

I am a music creator based in Pembrokeshire, Wales, specializing in music composition, for which I hold a Master's degree.

I focus primarily on contemporary music, including pop and electronic genres. I collaborate closely with other musicians and clients to create music tailored to their specific needs. I maintain an open mind towards composition.

My main interests in music include experimental pop, soundtrack composition, the relationship between math and music, and music as a form of expression. During my final year of undergraduate studies, I concentrated on the use of pop music in marginalized communities and its impact on society. I enjoy discussing music and the world surrounding it.
